Katrina Abad's Biography

Katrina Abad is an architecture major interested in participatory design, rehousing homeless populations, and representational methods. She is skilled in a variety of 3D modeling and drafting softwares like AutoCAD, Rhino 3D and SolidWorks, and has worked with Adobe Suite for over five years. She has a range of fabrication skills including basic carpentry, metal working, welding, and 3D printing as well as hand rendering and urban sketching. Her artwork has been shown in exhibitions in both Italy, New York and New Jersey and Katrina has experience as a freelance artist and window painter. In her architectural career she helped work on the exhibition design for architecture shows in different venues in NYC.

Personal Info

Outside of work, she enjoys drawing and making illustrations for her friends. During quarantine she expressed interest in making her own clothes and created several summer pieces as well as crocheting a few hats. She is a pescetarian but is allergic to fish, which Katrin explains may seem confusing but is actually quite simple. She has recently mastered the art of doing a headstand at will, and once did it while paddle boarding. However, she refuses to show anybody.
